About BISV CCSC

The Competitive Computer Science Club seeks to help students interested in Computer Science hone their skills and train for Competitive Programming competitions. With weekly practice competitions and lectures on useful algorithms for Competitive Computer Science, students will learn how to solve competitive programming problems and win competitions. This club teaches algorithmic, cryptography, and teamwork skills. We have gone on to compete in: SUMO ProCo, HPI, USACO, ACSL, and Google CodeJam.
